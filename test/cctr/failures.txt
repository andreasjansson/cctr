===
failing test reports failure count
===
cctr $CCTR_FIXTURE_DIR/failing --no-color 2>&1 | tail -1
---
Summary: 0 passed, 1 failed, 0 skipped in {{ t }}s
---
where
* t < 5

===
failing test exits non-zero
===
cctr $CCTR_FIXTURE_DIR/failing --no-color >/dev/null 2>&1; echo "exit: $?"
---
exit: 1
